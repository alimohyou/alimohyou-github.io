Honeypot

A cyber honeypot functions like a trap, luring hackers into a trap. It's a dummy computer system meant to draw cyberattacks, serving as a sacrifice. It imitates a hacker's target and takes advantage of their intrusion attempts to learn more about cybercriminals and their methods of operation, or to divert their attention from other targets.
