IoT Seminar

On December 5th an IoT seminar took place in EMu, School of Computing and Technology which have increase my knowledge and interest in IoT sector. An explaination about IoT was presented then followed with detailed demonstration about IoT platforms including MQTT, COAP and Rest API. Moreover, they stated that IoT challenges are cost, compatibility, security and connectivity issues. Top use cases of IoT were also stated including remote assets control, IoT based process automation, Vehicle fleet management, location tracking predective maintainance, IoT for plant performance and lastly on site track and trace .
