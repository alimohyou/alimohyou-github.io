Whois

Whois, which means "who is," enables anyone to search for the name and contact details of a domain name that has been registered (website). A popular Internet record listing called Whois tells you who owns a domain and how to contact them.

Whois command accepts website names. Command: whois google.com

Also accepts IP address. Command: whois 8.8.8.8


A you can see, all of the contact details for the individual, organisation, or business that registers a certain domain name are included in a Whois record. The name and contact details of the Registrant, or the person who owns the domain, the Registrar, or the company or individual that registered the domain name, the registration dates, the name servers, the most recent update, and the expiration date are typically included in each Whois record. The administrative and technical contact information (often, but not always, the registrant) may also be found in whois records.
